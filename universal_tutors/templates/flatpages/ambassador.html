Brand Ambassador Program

Help us promote shared learning in our online tutoring community!

What is a Campus Ambassador?

Wizoku is an online learning community that uses innovative technology to connect students and tutors. Our Campus Ambassadors help us build this community. Effective Ambassadors do this by being active on campus, promoting us on social media, and reaching out to both students and tutors.

Why should you be interested?

The more tutors and students ambassadors reach, the more money they make. We give Ambassadors their own code that allows us to track their progress and how many hours their recruits are teaching. We also provide them with a guide on how to be a great ambassador for wizoku, to make things easier.

Who are we looking for?

We are looking for students who are both motivated and involved on campus to help us spread the word. Strong ambassador applicants will be full-time students who are active on their campus, have a strong social media presence, and may also be interested in tutoring.

How it works?

  • $1/hour taught by one of your recruited tutors
  • $1/lesson hour attended by a recruited student
  • $10 bonus for every 50 tutored hours
  • $5 for referring an ambassador from your school
  • $10 for referring an ambassador from another school
  • Monthly rewards for the most active ambassadors
