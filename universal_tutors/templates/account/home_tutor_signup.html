Our Values

We believe all students deserve access to great teachers. Location should no longer determine the quality of teaching received. Everything we do is about challenging the status quo in terms of access to education.

Our Approach

Our goal is to put the focus of both off and online tutoring on quality personal relationships. By building an educational community of driven individuals, wizoku has created a new way to think about online education. Our belief in shared learning inspires our commitment to providing a dynamic educational resource.

Our Offering

Wizoku makes finding a great tutor simple. Whether learning in-person with a tutor around the corner, or online with someone from the other side of the world, our intuitive interface makes payment and scheduling easy.
