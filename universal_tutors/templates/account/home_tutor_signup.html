Our Values

All students deserve access to the tools and learning that will allow them to succeed. We believe that an open, collaborative community has the power to encourage learning opportunities everywhere. At Wizoku, we are committed to the idea that knowledge is a resource to which everyone should have access.

Our Approach

Our goal is to put the focus of tutoring on quality personal relationships. By building an educational community of driven individuals, wizoku has created a new way to think about online education. Our belief in shared learning inspires our commitment to providing a dynamic educational resource.

Our Offering

Our online community offers a unique platform for shared learning. The strength of this community is supported by great technology. Whether learning in-person with a tutor in your town, or in our online classroom with someone on the other side of the world, our intuitive interface makes payment and scheduling easy.
