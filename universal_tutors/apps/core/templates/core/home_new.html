What We Do

Our goal is to put the focus of online tutoring on quality personal relationships. By building a community of driven individuals seeking a great educational resource, Wizoku has created a new way to think about online education.

Our online classrooms provide the best available learning tools, including video, instant messenger, and an interactive whiteboard equipped with Wolfram Alpha an educational search engine. Wizoku’s intuitive interface makes payment and scheduling straightforward for parents, tutors, and students.

At Wizoku we work to provide you with an easy-to-use, yet comprehensive, resource for all of your learning needs. Please sign up and check out all of our features. We’d love to hear what you think, so don’t hesitate to give us feedback!
